GREENING DESERTS PROJECT

Every year, the area of deserts increases by an average of 10,000 square kilometers. Small villages suffer from dust storms and droughts, some have already been swallowed up by deserts, and now there is no life there, only sand. Big cities are also under threat now. We are thousands of volunteers from different cities and countries united by a common idea to prevent the spread of deserts.
